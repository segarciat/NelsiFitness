Welcome to Nelsi Pamela's macronutrient tool. The tool below makes use of the Harris-Benedict equations to estimate your daily caloric expenditure.

Instructions

  • Fill out every entry to enable the Calculate button.
  • Clicking Clear will remove your information from the form.
  • Clicking Save will save the information for future visits.
  • Click the Filter Users input to look through your saved users and access them.
  • Select a user to bring up their information.
  • Update a user's information by clicking Update.
  • If you don't wish to have a user's information stored for future visits, select that user and click Delete.
